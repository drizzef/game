Required Assets:

Images (place in assets/images/):
- player.png - Character image in pixel art style
- pipe.png - Obstacle pipe
- ground.png - Ground image
- background.png - Game background
- announcement.png - Envelope or letter image for the reveal
- pixel.png - 1x1 white pixel for particle effects (replace the text file)

Audio (place in assets/audio/):
- flap.wav - Sound when player jumps
- score.wav - Sound when scoring a point
- hit.wav - Sound when hitting an obstacle
- reveal.wav - Sound for the announcement reveal

You can create these assets using pixel art tools like Piskel (https://www.piskelapp.com/) or
use existing free pixel art assets and modify them.

For audio, you can find free game sounds on sites like freesound.org.
